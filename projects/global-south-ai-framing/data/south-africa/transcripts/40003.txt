Discussion
Mr B Herron (GOOD) asked whether the province had taken forward cover to mitigate the impact of fluctuations in the rand-dollar exchange rate on the cost of Microsoft licenses. Secondly, in relation to the R9 million surrendered due to unfilled posts, what did the term “core energy team” mean, and which positions had not been filled?
Premier Winde replied that the province did not currently take forward cover to mitigate currency fluctuations, but the DG or a team member could elaborate on the discussions with Microsoft. Stricter management systems were being put in place to minimise waste, such as unused licenses. The “core energy team” was a team established to bring together essential skills in the energy sector. It was led by Mr Alwie Lester, Special Advisor on Energy, Office of the Premier, and a budget to recruit specialists in areas like solar, wind, batteries, and green hydrogen had been allocated. Although some team members had been sourced from various departments, recruitment had been cautious to ensure funds were used effectively.
