### Key Innovation: Probing Questions System
Created `PROBING_QUESTIONS.md` - master index with escalation protocol:
- Level 1: Gentle probe
- Level 2: Explain why it matters
- Level 3: Challenge directly
- Level 4: Refuse to proceed

Each method has 5-7 probing questions that force explicit parameter choices. No silent defaults.

### Rigor Enforcement
