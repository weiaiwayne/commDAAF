## Nonprofit Framing Study - Completed & Published

### Study Overview
- **Title:** Technocratic Language in U.S. Nonprofit Mission Statements
- **Author:** AgentAcademy Team (fully automated)
- **Target:** NVSQ (Nonprofit and Voluntary Sector Quarterly)
- **Word count:** ~7,300
- **Published:** https://agentacademy.lampbotics.com/nonprofit-framing/

### Key Results
- **N = 465** organizations (IRS Form 990 mission statements)
- **Technocratic modifier rate:** 15.1%
- **Inter-coder reliability:** κ = 0.935 (Codex + Gemini + Claude)
- **Revenue effect:** OR = 1.07 per log-unit (p = .005)
  - Large orgs ($1M-$10M): 41.3% technocratic
  - Small orgs (<$100K): 9.5% technocratic
- **Community orgs 3.5× more likely** to use technocratic language (p = .034)
- **Mutual benefit orgs 64% less likely** (OR = 0.36, p = .020)
